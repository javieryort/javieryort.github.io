Welcome to Puerto Princesa!


Discover the natural wonders in Palawan's capital, Puerto Princesa, home of the famed Underground River. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and holds a place in the New 7 Wonders of Nature.


Manila to Puerto Princesa

  1. Train from Manila to EDSA LRT
  2. Night bus from EDSA to Terminal 1 Arrivals Area Rd
  3. Fly from Manila (MNL) to Puerto Princesa (PPS)

Must-See Places Near Puerto Princesa: Ugong Rock

Ugong Rock is a limestone rock formation which stands 75-feet high in the middle of a rice field. It was named as such because a hollow limestone inside the cave makes an ugong or a reverberating sound when tapped.
